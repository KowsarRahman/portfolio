My Ideal Job

For me, the ideal job would be a full stack developer working for a Consultancy firm or a startup.

Full Stack Developer

A full stack developer is a person who can take care of code from frontend till backend.

They are neither specialised on frontend nor specialised on backend. They can create designs for a website and simultaneously create APIs for a microservice. Also, they take care of testing units too and finally takes responsible for deploying the software or website.

It is appealing to me because I myself do not want to just only be the person in designing. My sixth sense will want to know how the backend behind this is working. Hence, I feel it apealing due to the fact that I can learn a lot out of it. Most importantly the industry needs a lot of full stack developer to fill the shortage and I feel I can be a part of it.

For a person to become a full stack developer, one needs to pick up a frontend framework first. This could be React, Vue or Angular. Then after this comes the backend part. It could be Java, Python, Ruby on Rails, etc. For frontend testing, one needs to know about Jest or Enzyme. As for backend, it could be JUnit for Java. Then for continous integration, one needs to be aware of CircleCI or Jenkins. Finally to deploy the application, a person can choose either AWS, Azure or GCP. All 3 of them are popular and are highly demanded by employers.

As of now I can code in React and have expertise in Spring Boot, Flask, etc. However I need to develop skills in Unit testing for both front and back end. I am also experience in AWS Cloud Computing however I plan to explore GCP and Azure as well.

Simply learning one front end framework is not enough. That is the reaosn why am I planning to learn Angular and Vue.js. I rely on Youtube and LinkedIn learning to know more about them. These days, I am preparing myself to prepare for the AWS Certified Cloud Practioner exam.
